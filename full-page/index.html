FAQs

Who all can register ?

  If you can innovate, you are eligible.

What are the registration fees ?

  Great hackathons don't make you pay.

How much time do I have to work on my idea?

  All participants can work for 24 hours on their project. The project proposals would be submitted prior to the hackathon, and the complete event is only for the developing stage.

How many members are required are in a team?

  No one likes to be a loner. You can come in as a couple, a trio or a quad. In simple words, a team size of 2-4 members.

Do I keep working throughout the event ?

  Of course not. IET does not like to make Jack a dully boy. We have numerous fun-filled mini-events in bag for you. Stay tuned to our FB page for detailed updates on the mini-events.

What if a participant is hungry ?

  Eat-Code-Drink are the only objectives of the hackathon. And we'll take care of the first and last for you. Pizzas, Coffee, Snacks...you name it!

Who are the judges for the event ?

  Only the best for you. The panel of judges include experienced proffessionals from various fields of technologies.

What are the prizes ?

  No one is a saint here. Everyone would be here to win. And the awards are to satisfy your need- cash prizes, goodies, certificates, and a lot more awaits you this HACK OFF.
